Pokemons

An orange, dragon-like Pokémon, Charizard is the evolved form of Charmeleon and the final evolution of Charmander. It also has two "Mega Evolved" forms, Mega Charizard X and Y

Squirtle is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1. Squirtle is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon. It has a blue body with purple eyes, a light brown belly, and a tough red-brown shell on its back.

Bulbasaur are small.A plant Pokémon that move on all four legs. They have blue-green bodies with darker blue-green spots. The seed on a Bulbasaur's back is planted at birth and then sprouts and grows along with it.

Mega Charizard X

Mega Charizard X is a black, muscular evolution of Charizard with a blue flame on its tail and emanating from its mouth, signifying its intense fire power; its body is sleek and toned with sharp, blue-tipped spikes on its shoulders, and when Mega Evolving, it gains a Dragon typing alongside its Fire type, making it a formidable aerial combatant.
